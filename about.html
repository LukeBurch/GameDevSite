About the Game

After the zombie apocalypse of 2030, humanity has taken shelter in the zombie bunkers. However, the Zombie Hoards are still out there and the player will need to defend the shelters from the left-over zombies, all while fighting to gather resources and help keep the hope alive that humanity will strive once again.

The game is based on a basic Zombie themed First person shooter game, this game is also taken insperation from games like titles, plague inc.
